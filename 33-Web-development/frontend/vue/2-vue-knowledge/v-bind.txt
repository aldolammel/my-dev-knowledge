

DIRECTIVE: V-BIND


    >> It's accepted for: any (with arg), obj (without arg).

    >> Examples:
        <img v-bind:src="p.image">
        Or shorter version: <img :src="p.image">

        Also:

        <div v-bind:class="{ selected : product.active }"
        Or shorter version: <div :class="{ selected : product.active }"
