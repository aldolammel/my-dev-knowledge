
REGISTERING YOUR APP MODELS (db tables) TO BE MANAGED BY DJANGO CMS:
    
    http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/

1) Go to 'admin.py' into your specific app folder, and register the desired model/table:
    
    # Import the specific table of the app:
    from .models import <class_name>
    
    # Now, register the table to the Django Admin CMS:
    admin.site.register(<class_name>)
