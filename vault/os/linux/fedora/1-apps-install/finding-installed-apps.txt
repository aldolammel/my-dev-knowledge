

FEDORA: FINDING INSTALLED APPS


        # Finding all:
            $ dnf list --installed
        
        
        # Finding all with 'adobe' in the name:
        
            $ dnf list --installed | grep adobe             <-- or "with quotes" for composed names.


        # Finding all with some 'obe' in the name:

            $ dnf list --installed | grep obe               <-- It'll find all Adobe stuff.


        # Finding all the initial letters are 'web':
        
            $ dnf list --installed | grep ^web
