

PROPS DRILLING: IN PROJECTS WITH DEEPER STRUCTURE, PROPS CAN BE VERY CONFUSING
    
    
    >> PROP DRILLING ISSUE - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

        When you need to pass data from a parent component down through multiple nested child components, you end up passing props through components that don't actually need the data - they're just middlemen.

        >> Prop drilling issue:

            <GreatGreatGreatGrandParent>
                <GreatGreatGrandParent :u="user">                                <!-- 1 lvl deep -->
                    <GreatGrandParent :u="user">                                 <!-- 2 lvl deep -->
                        <GrandParent :u="user">                                  <!-- 3 lvl deep -->
                            <Parent :u="user">                                   <!-- 4 lvl deep -->
                                <Child :u="user" />   <!-- Finally it uses the data.  5 lvl deep -->
                            </Parent>
                        </Grandparent>
                    </GreatGrandparent>
                </GreatGreatGrandparent>
            </GreatGreatGreatGrandparent>

            
            >> Project example of prop drilling issue:
                ./using-composition-api/z-examples/prop-drilling-example/example.vue
    

            >> Keep in mind:
                When the component that needs data is 4 level deep or more, consider to use Provide/Inject method!
